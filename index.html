Keys of the Endpoint is an illustrated fantasy serial written by TheKaizerWithin and drawn by Eln. All chapters except the newest are available for free online through this website. Just click on “Chapters” in the menu to start reading.

Keys of the Endpoint tells the story of how the first civilization in a fantastical dimension called the Endpoint came to be after a cataclysmic event consumed the previous civilizations. The Endpoint is a world marred by supernatural weather phenomena that periodically whisks away people, buildings and terrain from other dimensions and timelines.

The first arc concerns a man named Isac from Earth and his search for his lost brother, Finn, whom he suspects has been caught up in the whirlwind of a supernatural event. Because of his suspicions, Isac eventually discovers a way to enter the Endpoint and in so doing becomes, unbeknownst to him, the first to have entered the Endpoint intentionally.

In the Endpoint he stumbles upon Aster, a girl born in the Endpoint after the calamity, who struggles to regain some of her power after having it taken as ransom. Through a series of fortunate (and unfortunate) events, Isac manages to secure her loyalty and together they embark on a journey through the Endpoint to locate Isac’s long lost brother and regain Aster’s rightful ownership of her powers.

WATCH A CIVILIZATIONUNFURL

Watch a Civilization Unfurl

This story gives you a front seat to history. Watch as factions and coalitions form, battles take place and power structures fluctuate. Be witness to the consequences of each choice made by those in power. Experience the mixing of vastly different languages and cultures and see new ones form. Garner first hand knowledge of the origins of new traditions and customs as they form.

Fully Illustrated

Keys of the Endpoint is fully illustrated by the wonderful artist Eln. Give your imagination a helpful push in the right direction and imagine the rest on your own in your minds eye on top of Eln’s solid framework. Skip past excessive descriptions and dive right into the action. Approach the story from roughly the same visual viewpoint as everyone else, including the authors.

Cultivation & Progression Fantasy

Keys of the Endpoint has a heavy focus on personal growth, both as characters in terms of personal arcs but also in terms of combat strength. The magic in the Endpoint allows for gradual growth and interesting variations on that growth. Let the sense of satisfaction consume you as your favorite character collects and achieves ever greater heights of magical ability. Similar to series like Cradle by Will Wight and Arcane Ascension by Andrew Rowe, characters have their own unique flavor of motivation for pursuing strength but they all share one essential force that is forever pushing them towards an increasingly greater potential; survival.

Character Driven

Even though Keys of the Endpoint is about the birth of civilization, the whole story is told through the eyes of the characters and their lives as they struggle to survive and thrive in the chaotic Endpoint. All the main characters are exceptional or heroic in some way, but they all have flaws. Each character has one or more personal arcs they go through and come out on the other end, changed, not always for the better.
